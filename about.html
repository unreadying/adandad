about advertising & advertisements

ad&ad is a small toronto based advertising agency founded on the belief that everyone should advertise . there is obvious disparity between those who advertise and those who do not , so our goal is to provide substantial creative advertising that is accessible to all .

we respect small businesses and want to see them thrive . ad&ad understands the risks of advertising , so we keep costs low when working for small clients ; all costs go towards materials and adspace ; we only profit if you profit . we want small business to advertise ! we can do everything ! just contact us !





the current staff at ad&ad consists of two junior employees . the team shares interests in culture , sustainability and having a good time .
