our first client and long term partner , Sasha's Cars has been renting classic picture cars to the GTA since 2019 .

in early 2024 , we approached Sasha Fatsevych of Sasha's Cars . we had heard he wanted help growing his brand , so we prepared a pitch deck with some ideas and gave him a call . being focused on renting cars and growing his collection since the start , Sasha's internet presence was very minimal when we first started working with him ; wanting to give his brand a greater sense of legitimacy , we got to work on a website right away ( which you can check out here ) in addition to making regular posts on his instagram ( linked here ) featuring clients that had rented Sasha's Cars .

we know that Sasha's website and instagram are likely to be a clients first interaction with his brand , so we have been working to keep them informative and engaging . now that Sasha's Cars has an accessible first touch point for future clients , we are continuing our work for Sasha by creating advertisements . at the moment we are working on short form videos for his social media pages .
