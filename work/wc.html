resurrection

wilkins coffee decided it was time to re emerge into the world . that why they asked us to revive their classic "wilkins wontkins" campaign . watch the original campaign here . jim henson is credited with creating the commercials , which utilize his iconic puppets .

our approach

we wanted our campaign to follow the original closely , without being the exact same . to this end we decided to utilise our in house animator who was able to bring hensons characters to life in a brand new medium .

thanks to the fast paced short form style of the originals, we felt the classic formula would transfer well to digital avenues . with this in mind we began to ideate dozens of plots , before ultimately deciding to choose one and animate it . check that out below .







sadly , wilkins coffee no longer exists . this was merely a project assigned by professor Herman Pi'ikea Clark .
